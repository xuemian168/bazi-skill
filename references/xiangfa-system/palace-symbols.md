# Palace Symbols

Source basis: `SRC-PROJ-METHODS` position method and `SRC-PROJ-COMPAT` day-branch weighting. Use only after chart pillars are confirmed by code or by the user.

## Core Rules

### `XF-PALACE-YEAR-001`

- Position: year pillar.
- Symbol scope: family background, early environment, public/social context, ancestral or family narrative.
- Safe scene language: "早年环境", "家族/外部评价", "出身背景给出的惯性".
- Do not infer: exact family events, parental health, inherited wealth, or social class without additional evidence.

### `XF-PALACE-MONTH-001`

- Position: month pillar.
- Symbol scope: parents, youth development, career base, work environment, social role formation, strongest seasonal context.
- Safe scene language: "事业底盘", "工作组织", "成长阶段的规则和压力", "父母/长辈议题".
- Do not infer: a fixed occupation, employer type, or parent-specific event from month pillar alone.

### `XF-PALACE-DAY-001`

- Position: day stem and day branch.
- Symbol scope: day stem is self/day master; day branch is spouse palace, close relationship mode, inner living rhythm.
- Safe scene language: "亲密关系中的相处习惯", "内在安全感", "日常生活的舒适区".
- Do not infer: marriage certainty, divorce certainty, spouse personality as a fixed fact, or moral judgment.

### `XF-PALACE-HOUR-001`

- Position: hour pillar.
- Symbol scope: later life, children/subordinates, long-tail projects, legacy, future-facing plans.
- Safe scene language: "长期项目", "晚景/后半场", "子女或后辈议题", "长期资产与作品".
- Do not infer: exact number of children, fertility, child quality, or late-life outcome.

## Position Weighting

- Month branch carries season and environment weight; use it before small decorative symbols.
- Day branch carries high relationship weight in compatibility and marriage analysis.
- Hour pillar is low confidence when birth time, longitude, timezone, or true-solar-time correction is uncertain.
- Year pillar is useful for background tone, but should not dominate adult-life conclusions without Da Yun support.
