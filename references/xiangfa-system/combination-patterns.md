# Combination Pattern Symbols

Source basis: `SRC-PROJ-BZ`, `SRC-PROJ-METHODS`, and classical source-family provenance in `source-map.md`. Use only as local distilled heuristics; do not present these as exhaustive classical formulae.

## Common Patterns

### `XF-COMB-SHISHANG-CAI-001`

- Feature: 食伤 and 财星 form a visible/output-to-resource path.
- Scene: skill, writing, product, research, performance, or technical output can become resource, client value, or market value.
- Preconditions: computed ten gods show both output and resource features; position and strength decide confidence.
- Boundary: not a guarantee of wealth.

### `XF-COMB-GUAN-YIN-001`

- Feature: 官杀 and 印星 form pressure-to-support / rule-to-credential path.
- Scene: rules, exams, institutions, supervision, or pressure can be absorbed through learning, certificates, research, documents, or mentors.
- Preconditions: computed 官杀 and 印星 are present and not severely contradicted by supplied evidence.
- Boundary: not a guaranteed job title or official rank.

### `XF-COMB-SHA-YIN-001`

- Feature: 七杀 with 印星 support.
- Scene: high pressure becomes discipline, specialization, crisis handling, or professional training.
- Preconditions: 七杀 and 印星 are both confirmed; strength and position must be discussed.
- Boundary: do not romanticize chronic stress.

### `XF-COMB-SHANG-GUAN-JIAN-GUAN-001`

- Feature: 伤官 and 官星 tension.
- Scene: expression, critique, innovation, or technical autonomy rubs against authority, rules, review, or institutional process.
- Preconditions: computed 伤官/食伤 and 官星 are present; timing trigger raises confidence.
- Boundary: do not jump to lawsuit, job loss, or scandal.

### `XF-COMB-BI-JIE-CAI-001`

- Feature: 比劫 and 财星 tension.
- Scene: resources, clients, budgets, or money decisions are affected by peers, partners, siblings, competitors, or co-founders.
- Preconditions: computed 比劫 and 财星 are present; relation to useful/unfavorable elements matters.
- Boundary: do not guarantee loss; phrase as governance and boundary risk.

### `XF-COMB-CAI-YIN-001`

- Feature: 财星 and 印星 tension.
- Scene: practical money/resource demands compete with study, documentation, recovery, research, or elder/support systems.
- Preconditions: computed 财 and 印 are present; position/timing decides which side is foregrounded.
- Boundary: do not frame as "money destroys learning" deterministically.

## Use Discipline

- Use a pattern only when all required features are in the evidence packet.
- Mention whether the pattern is natal, Da Yun-triggered, Liu Nian-triggered, or event-window-triggered.
- Prefer one or two high-signal patterns over a long list.
