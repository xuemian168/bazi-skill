# Ten-God Symbols

Source basis: `SRC-PROJ-BZ` ten-god table and `SRC-PROJ-METHODS` ten-god semantic method. Use ten gods only when computed relative to the confirmed day stem.

## Rule Set

### `XF-TG-BI-JIE-001`

- Features: 比肩 / 劫财.
- Symbol scope: self-drive, peers, siblings, competitors, co-founders, same-level allies, resource sharing.
- Constructive image: independence, stamina, team resonance, willingness to compete.
- Risk image: rivalry, duplicated roles, resource splitting, stubborn self-protection.
- Safe wording: "同辈/合伙/竞争议题较容易被激活".

### `XF-TG-SHI-SHANG-001`

- Features: 食神 / 伤官.
- Symbol scope: output, expression, craft, technique, writing, performance, children themes, critique.
- Constructive image: publishing, teaching, productization, public expression, technical output.
- Risk image: over-speaking, challenging authority, perfectionistic critique.
- Safe wording: "更适合通过作品、表达、技术成果来打开局面".

### `XF-TG-CAI-001`

- Features: 正财 / 偏财.
- Symbol scope: resources, money handling, execution toward material goals, market exchange, spouse-symbol resonance in some male-chart readings.
- Constructive image: practical execution, budgets, assets, client/business awareness.
- Risk image: resource pressure, over-transactional choices, peer competition around money when 比劫 also strong.
- Safe wording: "财星更像资源经营和现实落地能力，不等于保证财富".

### `XF-TG-GUAN-SHA-001`

- Features: 正官 / 七杀.
- Symbol scope: rules, pressure, authority, responsibility, title, institution, discipline, spouse-symbol resonance in some female-chart readings.
- Constructive image: credential, role clarity, leadership under rules, professional seriousness.
- Risk image: anxiety under pressure, authority conflict, rigid standards, fear of mistakes.
- Safe wording: "官杀代表规则与压力，不应直接断成职位、婚姻或法律事件".

### `XF-TG-YIN-001`

- Features: 正印 / 偏印.
- Symbol scope: learning, documents, certificates, protection, elders, recovery, research, reflection.
- Constructive image: study, qualification, research support, documentation, mentoring.
- Risk image: overthinking, dependency on approval, delayed action, abstracting away from execution.
- Safe wording: "印星偏向学习与支持系统，强弱还需看位置、根气和运年".

## Zheng / Pian Nuance

- 正神 can be phrased as stable, formal, regular, and accountable when evidence supports it.
- 偏神 can be phrased as flexible, nonstandard, tactical, or fast-moving when evidence supports it.
- Do not treat 正 as automatically good or 偏 as automatically bad.
